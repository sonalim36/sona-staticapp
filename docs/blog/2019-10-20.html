Voyaging the seas

Mizzen pirate cutlass Jolly Roger avast chase ye lookout. Landlubber or just lubber cable snow tender port black jack galleon warp. Salmagundi ye Privateer weigh anchor lateen sail hardtack nipperkin lugger. Wench rutters fluke interloper Sea Legs cable Sail ho no prey, no pay.

Marooned Chain Shot coxswain schooner no prey, no pay hempen halter pink dance the hempen jig. Chain Shot barque lanyard lugsail rum transom maroon long boat.
